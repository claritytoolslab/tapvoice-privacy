2. Description of Service

TapVoice is an AI voice assistant for Apple Watch and iPhone. You ask a question by holding the TapVoice button on your Apple Watch, and the app returns a short spoken and written answer.

The app provides:

  • Free Trial: 10 questions total (lifetime limit) for new users
  • Question Packs: Consumable in-app purchases that add a fixed number of questions to your balance
  • Native support for 9 languages
  • Optional silent or full-audio response modes on Apple Watch
