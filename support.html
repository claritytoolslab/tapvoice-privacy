Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use TapVoice on my Apple Watch?

Simply open TapVoice on your Apple Watch, tap the microphone button, speak your question, and wait for the AI response. The answer will be displayed on screen and spoken aloud.

What subscription plans are available?

• Free: 3 questions total (lifetime)
• Starter: 240 questions/month + 60 web searches (€2.99/month)
• Pro: 750 questions/month + 150 web searches (€6.99/month)
• Max: 1,800 questions/month + 300 web searches (€12.99/month)

How do I enhance voice quality?

Go to Settings on your Apple Watch app, then General → Accessibility → VoiceOver → Speech → Voices. Download Enhanced quality voices for your preferred language for better audio experience.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! TapVoice stores all data locally on your devices. We don't collect personal information, track usage, or store your voice recordings. Questions are processed securely via OpenAI's API.

Can I use TapVoice without my iPhone?

Yes, TapVoice works independently on your Apple Watch when connected to Wi-Fi or cellular. Your iPhone doesn't need to be nearby for basic functionality.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → TapVoice → Cancel Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions through the App Store app.

Troubleshooting

Audio not working

  • Check that your Apple Watch volume is turned up
  • Ensure TapVoice has microphone permissions
  • Try restarting the TapVoice app
  • Verify your Apple Watch is connected to internet

Questions not processing

  • Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular)
  • Verify you haven't exceeded your monthly question limit
  • Speak clearly and avoid background noise
  • Try asking shorter, more direct questions

Subscription issues

  • Verify your subscription is active in iPhone Settings → Subscriptions
  • Try logging out and back into your Apple ID
  • Restart both your iPhone and Apple Watch
  • Contact Apple Support for billing-related issues

App crashes or freezes

  • Force close TapVoice and reopen it
  • Restart your Apple Watch by holding the side button
  • Update to the latest TapVoice version from the App Store
  • Ensure your Apple Watch has sufficient storage space
