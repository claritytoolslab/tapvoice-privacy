‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is feedback really anonymous?

Yes! We don't collect any personal information. The form only sends what you write. If you leave contact details blank, we don't know who you are.

Will I get a response to my feedback?

If you leave contact details, we'll respond when possible. If you want to stay anonymous, we can't reply.

How quickly is feedback processed?

We read all feedback personally. We usually respond within 2-3 days.

Can I send images or attachments?

This form doesn't support attachments. If you need to send images (e.g. in a bug report), contact us directly: tapvoiceai@gmail.com
